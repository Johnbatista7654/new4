Todin is a Web3 price comparison and shopping rewards platform that helps users save money while earning crypto. Our vision is to make online shopping smarter and more rewarding by combining traditional e-commerce with blockchain technology. We aggregate millions of products across multiple online stores, allowing users to compare prices, track wishlists, and set price alerts. On top of that, users earn cashback in $TDN and partner tokens, which can be redeemed, swapped, or used in our Todin Shopping Pool for BTC/USDT rewards. The problem we solve is twofold: For consumers: we save them money and reward them for shopping. For brands: we provide a powerful channel for customer acquisition through affiliate integrations, data insights, and tokenized loyalty. Our goal is to become the go-to shopping platform for both Web2 and Web3 users, bridging the gap between everyday shopping and crypto adoption.
